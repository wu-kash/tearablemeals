Tearable Meals

Introduction

Welcome to your next 50* meals. These Tearable Meals™ are simple recipes, easy to follow and all cramped up into a notepad.

No need to choose what you cook next, no long-winded stories, and no ads. Straight to the point, and straight to cooking your next Tearable Meal™.

How-To

  1. Tear off this front page.
  2. Place the page anywhere in your kitchen. Stick it to the walls, a countertop or anywhere you see fit. It should be easy to read.
  3. Your first recipe on the next page is waiting to be cooked.
  4. You are all set, enjoy the Tearable Meal™.
  5. Grab this page and throw it away.
